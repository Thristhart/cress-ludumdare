The goal of Cress is simple: get one of your pieces to the other side of the board. Once a player moves their piece off the board on the enemy's side, they win.

There are three basic actions in Cress.

Move

Pieces can move forward one square.
To move a piece, simply click on your piece and click a square ahead of it. Pieces cannot move onto already occupied squares.

Jump

Pieces can jump forward, left or right two squares.
However, pieces become "tired" after jumping, and cannot take an action for two turns after jumping.
To jump a piece, simply click on your piece and click a square to jump to. Pieces cannot jump onto already occupied squares.

Notice that the piece indicates how many turns are left until it is usable again.

Kill

Pieces can kill an enemy to their immediate left or right.
However, killing an enemy piece causes the killer to go back as far as possible in their row.
To kill a piece, simply click on your piece and click on an enemy to the left or right of it.
